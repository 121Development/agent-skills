# Output format

Use these exact section headings and ordering. Do not add introductions,
conclusions, or meta-commentary between sections.

---

## Metadata

- **Title:** [6-word label or actual title]
- **Author:** [name]
- **Medium:** [article | podcast | book chapter | lecture | paper | other]
- **Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD or "unknown"]

---

## High-impact ideas

1. [16 words max]
2. [16 words max]
...

Target: 20-50 ideas. If the content is short, 20 is fine. If dense, push to 50.

## Abstract insights
